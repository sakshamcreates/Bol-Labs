Why BOL is different from a mirror practice.

Mirrors don't give feedback. Friends sugarcoat. BOL tells you exactly how your speaking sounds — in numbers and simple language — so you know what to fix.

Practice like it's the real stage.

30–60 second focused drills. Pick a topic, hit record, and speak. BOL treats every attempt like a mini-presentation — not casual timepass — so your brain learns under pressure, just like the real viva or pitch.

Use the timer and audio playback to simulate stage stress and improve under realistic conditions.

Instant, honest feedback.

No buttering, only breakdown. You get a confidence score plus a breakdown of timing, pauses and average loudness. You see why you sound underconfident — not just a random number that tells you nothing.

Feedback is designed to be actionable: one tweak per attempt so progress compounds fast.

Built for students & speakers.

Whether it's a college viva, placement interview, or tech-fest pitch, BOL helps you test your delivery before you walk into the room — so you don't freeze when it actually matters.

Works on laptop or phone: quick daily drills fit into study schedules and do not require a coach.

How this prototype actually works.

This is not a random-number generator. The demo analyzes measurable parts of your audio in the browser — then converts them into a confidence estimate.

Step 1
You speak for 30–60 seconds.

Talk about yourself, your project, or any topic. BOL records your voice, tracks how long you speak, and when you pause — just like a real viva or HR round.

Step 2
We analyze timing, pauses & volume.

The system calculates speaking length, silence gaps, and average loudness. These are combined in a scoring formula to estimate your confidence level on a 0–100 scale.

Step 3
You get a score and clear next steps.

You see your confidence score plus plain-language advice: slow down, reduce fillers, increase volume, or pause less — so every attempt becomes one step closer to stage-ready.

Future: from prototype to full AI speaking coach.

This tech-fest version is a working prototype. The long-term vision is a full-stack AI coach that tracks your progress over months and prepares you for any stage.

  • Emotion & stress detection directly from your voice (so the app notices panic before you do).
  • Filler-word detection like "umm", "uhh", "aa", "that", "actually", "I like".
  • Personalized practice plans for interviews, group discussions, and presentations.
  • Mobile app for daily 5-minute speaking workouts and streak-based progress tracking.
