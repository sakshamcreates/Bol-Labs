✦ Founder Note
Built by Saksham Singh

BOL started with a simple observation: most students practice speaking blindly. They rehearse, they present, they guess at what went wrong — and repeat.

The goal was to create a platform that measures delivery, confidence, pauses, pacing, and speaking consistency using real data instead of guesswork.

Every release in this changelog reflects progress toward that mission.
