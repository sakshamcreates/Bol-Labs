Full Policy

BOL is committed to transparency. Here is the complete breakdown of how your data is handled.

01
📋
Information We Collect
When you use BOL, we collect the following categories of information to power your experience:
Account information — name, email address, and authentication credentials when you register.
Audio recordings — voice input captured during practice sessions to perform analysis.
Speech transcripts — text generated from your spoken sessions.
Confidence scores — numeric results and session metrics produced during analysis.
Usage analytics — interaction patterns, session frequency, and platform activity.
02
💡
How We Use Information
The data BOL collects is used exclusively to deliver and improve your speaking confidence experience:
Generate real-time confidence analysis across pace, pauses, and vocal stability.
Produce personalised speaking feedback and identify specific areas to improve.
Enhance platform features, algorithms, and analysis accuracy over time.
Provide user support, respond to inquiries, and resolve account issues.
03
🎚️
Audio Processing
BOL is built around your voice. When you start a session, your microphone input is captured directly in your browser and sent to our processing pipeline. This audio may be processed to generate transcripts, calculate speech pace and pause metrics, evaluate vocal stability, and produce a numeric confidence score. Audio data is used strictly to deliver session results and improve analysis accuracy. BOL does not use your voice recordings for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to speaking analysis. Sessions are processed securely, and data is not shared with third parties beyond the services listed below.
04
🔗
Third-Party Services
BOL integrates with a limited set of trusted third-party providers to deliver its core functionality. These services may process certain data in accordance with their own privacy policies.
OpenAI APIs Speech Processing Analytics Services
OpenAI APIs may process transcripts to generate analysis and feedback.
Speech processing services handle audio-to-text conversion.
Analytics services help us understand platform usage at an aggregate level.
05
🛡️
Data Storage & Security
BOL takes reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is stored on secure infrastructure and accessed only by authorised processes and personnel where necessary. While we implement industry-standard protections, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be entirely secure. We encourage users to practise safe account hygiene, including using strong, unique passwords and logging out after sessions on shared devices.
06
✅
Your Rights
You have meaningful control over your data. At any time, you may exercise the following rights by contacting us directly:
Access your data — request a copy of the information BOL holds about you.
Correct your information — request updates or corrections to inaccurate data.
Request deletion — ask that your account and associated data be permanently removed.
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sakshamsingh1940@gmail.com.
07
🍪
Cookies & Analytics
BOL uses cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session state, remember preferences, and understand how the platform is used. Analytics data is collected to identify which features are most valuable, detect errors, and guide product decisions. This data is processed in aggregate form and is not used to build individual advertising profiles. You may manage cookie behaviour through your browser settings, though doing so may affect platform functionality.
08
🔄

When material updates occur, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of BOL after changes have been published constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms. Where significant changes affect how your data is used, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users directly.
